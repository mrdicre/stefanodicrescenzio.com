Fabled by Marie Claire

Lead UX Designer

Beauty as you know it has changed forever, Fabled by Marie Claire – a unique beauty and wellness destination in collaboration with Ocado – has finally launched and it’s set to become the one-stop-beauty-shop for beauty junkies everywhere.

Fables are stories that have withstood the test of time and been passed from generation to generation. Marie Claire has 27 years' experience in telling great beauty stories and we're here to inspire, educate and advise. Selected by our Beauty Experts and curated by Marie Claire, we introduce you to the Marie Claire Edit – your destination for the latest looks, trends, techniques and insider knowledge.
